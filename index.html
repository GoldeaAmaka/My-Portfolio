About

I am a developing Data Analyst with growing hands-on skills in SQL, Excel, Python, and Power BI, and a strong interest in reporting, dashboarding, and data-driven decision support. I am currently studying BSc (Hons) Digital Marketing & Data Analytics at York St John University, where I have built a solid foundation in data cleaning, analysis, modelling, and visualisation. I have completed certifications in SQL, PowerBI and Excel and am actively progressing through the 100 Days of Code (Python) programme, strengthening my ability to work confidently with structured datasets and analytical workflows. I am particularly interested in data analytics and I enjoy transforming raw, complex data into clear, actionable insights, developing accessible dashboards, and creating well-structured reports that support team and stakeholder decision-making. My work emphasises data accuracy, consistency, and clear communication, ensuring insights are reliable and easy to understand for non-technical audiences.

As I continue to grow, I am focused on strengthening my ability to define key metrics and KPIs, analyse trends and patterns, and present findings through visualisations, dashboards, and concise reporting. Alongside my core analytics skills, I also have experience with Python automation, API integrations (Spotify, Google), and building small data-driven web applications using Flask, HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap, which support my ability to create practical analytical tools when required. I am committed to continuous learning and eager to contribute to data-driven organisations where reporting, insight generation, and business impact are central. I am open to data analyst internships, junior / entry-level BI roles, reporting-focused projects, and collaborative opportunities where I can apply my skills to real business problems and continue developing as a professional analyst.
